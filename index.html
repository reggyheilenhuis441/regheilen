5 Best Investment Banking Courses Online [2020]

1. The Complete Investment Banking Course 2019 (Udemy)

Anyone interested in the Wall Street figures can enroll for this course on Investment Banking that has a comprehensive set of animation video tutorials and interactive real-life exercises. The course is curated by 365 Careers, and they are one of the leading and best-selling finance course providers on Udemy. Students will learn the fundamentals of investment banking along with advanced level lectures that will help them to land a job in the finance sector. You will be able to explore real-life case studies of companies and learn valuations and investment tactics.

 

Key USPs –

– Learn about bonds, equities, company valuations, IPOs

– Become a Udemy Certified Investment Banker

– Know how to draft IPOs and the ins and outs IPO pricing

– Learn company restructuring and asset management

– Understand the operations of the world’s topmost companies with their valuation and buyout calculations

 

Duration: Self-paced

Rating: 4.5 out of 5

2. Investment Banking Training (CFI)

The e-learning training provided by CFI is meant to educate investment banking associates and analysts and those who want to pursue a career in this field. This training will enable those who sign up for it with the technical knowledge of financial modeling and also help with networking skills. This course has a lot of theoretical and practice material for everyone to learn and apply. Once you complete the training, you get a certification making you an Investment Banking Analyst.

 

Key USPs –

– Know the basics and fundamental concepts of investment banking

– Learn about finance modeling, company valuations, and corporate finances

– Understand banking statement analysis and master your excel using aptitude

– Know about M&A modeling to understand company structures in detail and the financial management within

 

Duration: 6 weeks

Rating: 4.4 out of 5

3. Investment Banking Certification (New York Institute of Finance)

Work in the IBC role of your choice after getting sufficient knowledge about the role of financial management in business. This is a foundational certification course by the New York Institute of Finance and is designed to help the finance buffs around the world to get trained and become certified professionals. It is curated to be equivalent to mini-MBA graduation at a low cost. Once you complete this course, you can work with financial firms to provide your expertise as an IBC holder.

 

Key USPs –

– Finance management and statement analysis for managers

– Information about mutual funds, equity, and hedge funds

– Know company structure and M&A deal drafting

– Understand risk management in businesses

– Gain knowledge about Wealth management and Portfolio management

 

Duration: Self-paced

Rating: 4.3 out of 5

You can Sign up Here

 

Review: Become an IBC holder by enrolling for this financial training and learn about business finances to work with the top finance firms of the world.

 

 

4. Free Investment Banking Course (WallStreetMojo)

Get free training for investment banking from WallStreet Mojo with video tutorials and guides. This course will run you through the basics of financial learning, including accounting, excel skills, company valuations, financial modeling, and networking. Master the key aspects of Investment Banking and start practicing to expand your career horizons. Apart from the course, you also get a certificate of doing the course and you can add it to your portfolio.

 

Key USPs –

– Get free access to video and course guide material to learn Investment Banking

– Master your accounting and excel sheet handling skills to make financial calculations

– Learn IPOs and equity investments to know how stocks work
